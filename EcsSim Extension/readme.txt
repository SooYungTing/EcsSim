EcsSim COMP1202 Programming 1 Coursework

Description:

- EcsSim is a program that is designed to simulate various aspect of a university.
- This simulation covers activities such as building or upgrading the facilities, managing the budgets, hiring staffs and assigning students to each staff member.
- The program prints out everything that has been happening over the specified years out on a file called EcsSimOutput.txt

Features:
- Simulating a university operation for years
- During the simulation the budget and reputation will be managed
- Staffs will be hired from the staff market based on their skills and be allocated to students
- the university will build or upgrade facilities like the labs, halls, libraries and theatres
- at the end of the year, the maintenance cost and total staff salary will be deducted from the budget
